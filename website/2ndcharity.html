Our other favourite charity is the British heart foundation

Why?

  • They are the UK number one leading heart charity
  • Over 50 years of cutting research
  • They have already made a change in peoples lives

Their work

Today, They are funding thousands of research projects around the UK that are fighting heart disease.preventing heart disease, tests, conditions, treatments and life saving skills.

Their publications offer vital information for heart patients and they also have resources for healthcare professionals. They work hard to protect heart health and fight for better services for heart patients. Find out about the campaigns and how you can make a difference. They are also taking our fight to politicians, government officials, workplaces and schools, But they can't do it alone. they are working alongside government, other health charities, healthcare professionals and thousands of dedicated supporters. Everybody has a part to play in the fight against heart disease.
